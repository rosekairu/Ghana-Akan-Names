A K A N NAME GENERATOR

Generate Your Akan Name

Akan Names are derived from the Akan people of Ghana who frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

This is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.
